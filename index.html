About Us

We are the MDS Sport mobile app development team, united by a shared love for sports and everything related to it. We create solutions for sports fans, helping them stay closer to their favorite teams, conveniently follow important news, make decisions based on collected statistics, and simply have a great time with our interactive features and fantasy sports. Our mission is to unite and sustain interest in sports.

Playmaker AI

All Sports Statistics in One Pocket

Playmaker is a multifunctional sports app. Choose your sport and favorite teams to receive updates on upcoming matches, complete game and player statistics, victory predictions, and betting odds. Enter your bets in our tracker, monitor your personal statistics, and share your successes with friends.

Soccer News

Smart News About Your Favorite Team

The Soccer News app makes it easy to find and follow news about your favorite team. Simply select your team, and you can receive a daily summary of what happened. You can listen to the news or go to the original source to read the full text, share your favorite news with friends, or save it to read later. If you wish, you can customize the number of favorite teams and news sources. Download and join fans from all over the world.
